Day 1: Arrival & Setup Arrive 10 PM

Take a taxi or train (15 min) from Nice Côte d'Azur Airport to Cagnes-sur-Mer.

Day 2: The Corniche Loop 102 km

102kmDist
1,850mClimb

Route: Menton - Col de Castillon - Sospel. Classic alpine coastal mix.

Download GPX
Afternoon: Visit the medieval village of Historic Haut-de-Cagnes and the Château Grimaldi museum. Dinner at Le Grimaldi.

Day 3: Gorges du Loup 115 km

115kmDist
1,650mClimb

Route: Inland via Cagnes and Vence to the spectacular Gorges and Gourdon.

Download GPX
Afternoon: Musée Renoir, surrounded by ancient olive trees. Dinner: La Maison de la Pêche (Fresh Seafood).

Day 4: Esterel Red Rocks 98 km

98kmDist
900mClimb
Route: Cagnes → Cannes → Corniche d'Or → Agay → Col du Testanier → DN7 → Cagnes. Download GPX
Afternoon: Relax on the pebble beaches. Dinner at L'Atelier for creative bistronomy.

Day 5: The Madone Benchmark 88 km

88kmDist
2,100mClimb

Route: Cagnes - Nice - Menton - Col de la Madone - Peille. High intensity.

Download GPX
Afternoon: Visit the famous artistic village of Saint-Paul-de-Vence, just a short climb away.

Day 6: The Queen Stage 122 km

122kmDist
2,600mClimb

Route: The mighty Col de Turini loop. Pure mountain terrain.

Download GPX
Afternoon: Full rest. Sunset walk along the Cros-de-Cagnes Promenade.

Day 7: Var Valley Silent Peaks 105 km

105kmDist
1,700mClimb

Route: Exploring the rugged, quiet roads of Gilette and Utelle.

Afternoon: Polygone Riviera (Open-air shopping & dining).

Day 8: Italian Border Loop 110 km

110kmDist
1,800mClimb

Route: Across to Italy for coffee in Dolceacqua, then back via Col d'Èze.

Afternoon: Wine tasting at Château de Crémat.

Day 9: Final Panoramic Loop 95 km

95kmDist
1,900mClimb

Route: Col de Vence and a final steep kick up Mont Chauve. Gear check at Eat Sleep Cycle Hub.

Afternoon: Souvenir hunting in Vieux Nice. Dinner at Le Charlot (Cagnes Seafront).

Day 10: Departure Flight 12 PM

Final pack-down. Train from Gare de Cagnes-sur-Mer (12 mins) or Taxi to Nice Côte d'Azur Airport.
